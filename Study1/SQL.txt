NoSQL 的分类

key-value, 如 Redis
document based 如 CouchDB, Elasticsearch, MongoDB

NoSQL 特点和优势
1. Performance, because no need to join table, no restriction
1, 一般不支持ACID特性；
2, 无需经过sql层的解析，读写性能很高；
3, 数据没有偶合性，扩展性强


Relationship DB
* Maintainance high cost, index, consistence
* Scalability not convience
